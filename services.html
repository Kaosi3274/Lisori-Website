Our Services

Complete multimedia support for professional events.

From screen rental and sound setup to lighting, display support, and installation, 234-Multimedia helps events run smoothly from planning to delivery.

01

LED Screen Rental

We provide high-quality LED screen rental for conferences, exhibitions, concerts, church events, corporate functions, product launches, and outdoor events.

Our LED display solutions are suitable for main stages, side screens, sponsor displays, exhibition booths, overflow areas, registration points, and audience engagement.

02

LED Wall Setup & Installation

We provide professional LED wall setup for temporary events and permanent installations. Our team handles screen configuration, mounting support, display setup, testing, and technical operation to ensure clear and stable visual output.

03

Sound System Rental

234-Multimedia provides professional sound reinforcement for events of different sizes. From corporate conferences to concerts and church programs, we support clear audio delivery with suitable sound systems, microphones, monitors, and technical support.

04

Stage Lighting

We provide stage lighting solutions that enhance visibility, atmosphere, and event presentation. Our lighting support is suitable for conferences, concerts, award ceremonies, church programs, and corporate productions.

05

Conference & Exhibition Support

We support conferences, summits, exhibitions, and trade shows with LED screens, sound, media display, technical crew, and on-site support. Our team helps ensure presentations, branding, videos, and live event visuals are delivered professionally.

06

Event Multimedia Support

We provide multimedia support for events that require professional display, content playback, visual coordination, and technical handling. This includes screen management, presentation display, video playback, sponsor branding, and event visual support.

07

Permanent LED Screen Installation

We support organizations that require permanent LED display solutions for halls, churches, offices, event centers, auditoriums, and public spaces. Our team can assist with screen planning, installation, configuration, and technical support.

08

Technical Crew & On-Site Support

Beyond equipment, we provide trained technical personnel to manage setup, testing, operation, troubleshooting, and breakdown during events. This ensures the event runs smoothly and technical issues are handled quickly.
