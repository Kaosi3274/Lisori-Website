Our Services

Complete multi-service solutions for practical project needs.

From ICT setup and solar installation to CCTV security, real estate support, pharmaceutical products, training, and general contracts, LISORI LTD helps clients get dependable service from planning to delivery.

01

ICT Hardware and Software Infrastructure

We provide ICT solutions that help businesses and organizations operate efficiently in today's digital environment.

Our services include computer hardware supply and installation, software configuration, office IT setup, networking, internet infrastructure, server and data management support, consultancy, maintenance, troubleshooting, and ICT training.

02

Solar Energy Power Installation and Training

We design and install solar power systems for homes, offices, schools, businesses, and organizations that need reliable alternative energy.

Our support includes solar panel installation, inverter and battery setup, energy assessment, hybrid power solutions, maintenance, renewable energy consultation, and practical solar installation training.

03

CCTV Camera and Security Installations

We install surveillance and security systems for homes, offices, estates, commercial buildings, and institutional environments.

Our services include CCTV camera installation, IP and analog camera setup, DVR/NVR configuration, remote viewing setup, access control support, security maintenance, and security consultation.

04

Real Estate Services

We assist individuals, property owners, investors, and organizations with property-related decisions and support.

Our real estate services include property sourcing, sales and rental support, consultation, property management support, land and housing advisory, facility support, and connecting property owners with potential clients.

05

Pharmaceutical Products & Services

LISORI LTD also provides pharmaceutical products and related support services to individuals, businesses, healthcare providers, and organizations.

Our pharmaceutical services include healthcare product distribution, medical and wellness product sourcing, hospital and pharmacy-related item supply, general pharmaceutical procurement, consultation, and product supply support.

06

Training and Consultancy

We provide practical training and advisory support for individuals and organizations that want to build knowledge in ICT, solar energy, security systems, and related technical areas.

07

Maintenance and Support

We help clients maintain installed systems, troubleshoot issues, improve existing setups, and keep ICT, solar, and security solutions working reliably.

08

Project Coordination

For contract-based work, we support planning, sourcing, execution, communication, and coordination so projects are handled professionally and transparently.

09

General Contracts

LISORI LTD undertakes general contract services across different sectors for individuals, private businesses, corporate organizations, and institutions.

We support procurement and supply, project execution, facility support, business support services, technical service contracts, installation and maintenance contracts, consultancy, and project coordination.
