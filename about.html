About LISORI LTD

Reliable solutions for technology, energy, security, property, and contracts.

LISORI LTD is a multi-service solutions company based in Abuja, Nigeria. We provide professional services in ICT infrastructure, solar energy installation, CCTV and security systems, real estate services, pharmaceutical products, training, and general contracts.

We support individuals, businesses, institutions, and organizations with reliable technology solutions, energy systems, security installations, property-related services, and contract execution. Our focus is to deliver dependable, practical, and cost-effective solutions that improve productivity, security, comfort, and operational efficiency.

At LISORI LTD, we combine technical knowledge, professional service delivery, and customer-focused support to ensure every project is handled with quality, reliability, and attention to detail.
