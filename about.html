About 234-Multimedia

Reliable multimedia support for professional events.

234-Multimedia is an event multimedia and technical production company providing LED screen rental, sound, lighting, and visual display support for professional events. Based in Abuja, we support organizations, event producers, corporate bodies, government agencies, churches, and brands with reliable multimedia solutions for events of different scales. Our work is driven by a clear understanding of what successful events require: dependable equipment, proper planning, skilled technicians, and smooth execution. We are committed to helping clients deliver events that are visually engaging, professionally managed, and technically reliable.
